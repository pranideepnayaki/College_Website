Placements

"Recession?" Our Placement Cell is not daunted by this word. The two placement officers on board have left no stone unturned to ensure a placement record which is a source of pride to Geethanjali College of Engineering & Technology.

The Centre plays a vital role in the college set up in forging fruitful and mutually beneficial linkages with industries and professional organizations. Working effectively and efficiently, it liaises with various industries and associations to facilitate training, getting live projects as well as providing placement assistance to its students. The department also takes care to ensure an active interaction with the industry by organizing key guest lectures and seminars.

Placement for us constitutes an important means of fulfilling a key part of our mission: helping the industry and society by offering them, for free, world class research skilled information technology resources they need most. Landing into a dream job after completing their studies is what most of the students aim at.

Training & Placement activities had great successes at GCET and been continuously striving towards maximizing the percentage of placements. Our students have been placed in the best organizations like Satyam, Infosys, Birla soft, IBM, Wipro, TCS, Value Labs, Virtuasa, Applabs, Cognizant, Patni Computers, SQL Star, AMR Infosys, TMI Network, GE, NIC, Aryan Infoway, Six Sigma, Data Monitor, Capital IQ, Assurgent Technologies, HSBC, GE Capital, DELL, CFC International, Sutherland, Unitech, CMC, Hexaware, Infotech, CSC etc.

This year GCET students are selected in ISRO, Infosys, TCS, Accenture, Mahindra Satyam, Vikram Sarabhai Space Research Centre, Gulf Oil Corporation Limited, Reddy Labs & other Chemical, Varnish and Pharmaceuticals, Wipro BPO, Infosys BPO etc.

The dedication and hard work of the team supplemented by the cooperation and indispensable support from the authorities bore productive results.
